FAQ

  • How can I apply?
    The application for bachelor programs is done via the Mannheim University of Applied Sciences. For this you have to apply via hochschulstart.de.
    After completing your application, submit the application online.
    The application for master programs is done via the Mannheim University of Applied Sciences.
    For more information on the application procedure for your desired degree program, please click above to select the desired degree above and then selecting the desired degree program.
  • When is the online application available?
    For the winter semester 2021, you can apply online for the bachelor's degree programs starting June 1, 2021. apply.
    For the Master's programs, you can apply from May 15, 2021 or June 1, 2021.
  • When is the application deadline?
    The application with the complete documents must be received at the Mannheim University of Applied Sciences by the following deadlines. have been received:
    • For the summer semester by January 15 (Bachelor and Master)
    • For the winter semester (Bachelor) by July 31
    • For the winter semester (Master) until July 15
  • What admission requirements do I need to meet?
    You can apply with the successful completion of the general university entrance qualification, university of applied sciences entrance qualification or subject-linked university entrance qualification.
    In addition, professionals with successful completion of a master craftsman's examination or a professional further training can also apply. Further explanations can be found at Access for Professionals.
  • How often can I apply?
    You can apply to a maximum of three different programs.
  • What documents should be included with the application?
    Once you have completed your online application, all of the documents listed that you should submit with your application.
  • Can I apply for a higher semester?
    It is generally possible to apply for a higher semester, if you have completed your undergraduate studies. The corresponding faculty then decides whether achievements that have already been made can be credited and in which semester you can be admitted. can be accepted.
